Background

In 2022, I participated in a 48-hour product design hackathon. The challenge was to redesign the Air-Venti app, which aimed to help users track the availability of ICU beds and ventilators. This app in its current form, does not provide a good user experience.

Objectives

Primary Objective:
Redesign the user experience for users of all age groups.

Secondary Objective:
Design a map view displaying hospital locations with ICU bed and ventilator statistics.
