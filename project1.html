Problem Statement

Navigating the intricate process of college placement is a challenge for both students and employers. To simplify this journey, the goal was to create a comprehensive College Placement Website. The website aimed to streamline the placement process and enhance the overall user experience.

Background

Traditional placement methods were often cumbersome, involving paperwork and lengthy procedures.

Objectives

  • Student Registration
  • Interactive Chat Support
  • Secure Login Page
  • Efficiency and Accessibility
