Problem Statement

Navigating the intricate process of college placement is a challenge for both students and employers. To simplify this journey, the goal was to create a comprehensive College Placement Website. The website aimed to streamline the placement process and enhance the overall user experience.

Research

User Research: Summary

A survey was conducted targeting 3rd and 4th year students, identifying essential features such as real-time notifications, automatic CV builder, and a chatbot for resolving queries.
  • Out of the 24 people, 70% were from final year and 30% were from pre-final year
  • 91.7% found the CV verification process time-consuming and tedious
  • 83.3% felt that these activities could have been done online
